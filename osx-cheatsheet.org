* OS X Yosemite
** Mounting/unmounting drives
Get drive identifier from the output of:
#+BEGIN_SRC 
diskutil list
#+END_SRC
- eg, =/dev/disk3s1=

Mount:
#+BEGIN_SRC 
diskutil mount /dev/disk3s1
#+END_SRC

Unmount:
#+BEGIN_SRC 
diskutil unmount /dev/disk3s1
#+END_SRC
